Neill Blomkamp (born 17 September 1979) is a South African-Canadian film director.
He is also a film producer, screenwriter, and animator.
Blomkamp employs a documentary-style, hand-held, cinéma vérité technique.
He is also known for blending naturalistic and photo-realistic computer-generated effects.
He is best known as a co-writer and director of a critically acclaimed film.
